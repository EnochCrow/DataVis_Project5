World Trade During the WW2 Era (1938-1950)

The historical trade data was obtained from Correlates of War. The lists of Allied and Axis Powers were obtained from Wikipedia. The graphics were made with Observable Plot. The force directed graph template was obtained from D3 on Observable Plot. I used the following website to get the colors: IMAGECOLORPICKER. The dataset was filtered with python with help from Chat-GPT .

I cut down the data to just the main Allied and Axis Powers to make it more manageable to visualize. You can view the change in trade during the war period by adjusting the slider. Countries may not be named as they were during the period (Russia vs. USSR), the country names were assigned by the Correlates of War team.

Hypothesis: Trade between Allied and Axis Powers dropped significantly due to the outbreak of hostilities in WW2.
