Global Population

The population data was taken from Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development. The choropleth template was taken from an Observable Notebook at D3 Choropleth The graphics were made with Observable Plot. The stacked horizontal bar chart was largely taken from here, before modifications D3 on Observable Plot. The Observable template/files for the World Map were taken from d3 World Map. I used the following website to get the colors: Adobe Color. The dataset was filtered with d3/javascript with help from Chat-GPT .

I realized while processing the data that it was much more limited than I initially thought, with only around 40 countries included. You can choose the Year to view with the drop down menu, and you can pick which Country to see a distribution from by clicking on the map. I also included an option to view the bar graph with one set scale across all countries and years.

Hypothesis: Developed countries have faced an increasing problem with Aging populations over the past 20 years.
